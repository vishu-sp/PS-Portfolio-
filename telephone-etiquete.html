Essential Telephone Guidelines

When Answering Calls

  • Answer within 2-3 rings
  • Identify yourself and your department
  • Speak clearly and professionally
  • Maintain a positive tone

Call Handling

  • Always ask permission before placing on hold
  • Check back with caller every 30-45 seconds
  • Provide options if hold will be extended
  • Thank caller for their patience

Message Management

  • Record complete contact information
  • Note date, time, and urgency
  • Deliver messages promptly
  • Maintain message confidentiality
